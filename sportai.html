SportAI

SportAI is a sports analytics tool to democratize fantasy sports. It's a different take on a fantasy sports apps. Whereas most pack in as much information into single screens as much as possible, we designed SportAI with a different approach.

Aside from design, SportAI's main selling point is its backend machine learning algorithm which aims to predict athlete performance before their games.

I was the first designer at SportAI. I joined a team of 3 to revamp their design and build out the look and feel of the app.
