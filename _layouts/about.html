About Me
How I Got Here and Where I Wanna Go

As far back as I can remember, I always wanted to be a creator.

When I was ten, I drew cartoon portraits of my friends in class. They weren't good, but they were unique.

When I was fifteen, I found Instagram and got into photography. Two of my shots were featured on an SXSW-exclusive can of Brisk Iced Tea.

My creative interests changed constantly growing up, but all along, great software accompanied me.

I believe the only way to know how to create fantastic software is to use it as much as possible.
