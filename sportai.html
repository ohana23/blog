SportAI

SportAI is a sports analytics tool to democratize fantasy sports. It's a different take on fantasy sports apps. Whereas most pack as much information as possible into a single screen, we designed SportAI with a different approach.

Here, I talk about the design process and thinking behind the way we began desigining our initial version of the app.

Starting from scratch

SportAI is a sports analytics tool to democratize fantasy sports. It's a different take on fantasy sports apps. Whereas most pack in as much information into single screens as much as possible, we designed SportAI with a different approach.

Aside from design, SportAI's main selling point is its backend machine learning algorithm which aims to predict athlete performance before their games.

I was the first designer at SportAI. I joined a team of 3 to revamp their design and build out the look and feel of the app.
